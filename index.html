Welcome

Welcome to my page! I go by the name of Alex but my actual name is Wojciech Aleksander Wikiel. I took on the name Alex when I emigrated from Warsaw, Poland as a child. I moved to Buffalo, New York at the age of six and came to my current home state of Rhode Island at the age of 13. Decades later, I am still here raising my own family and “living the dream” as some might say. Across this page, you will find more content about myself and my interests. Maybe you will find something we have in common or discover something new to you. Thanks for visiting!
